About Us

Len Lelevich

Len has worked in the construction industry for over twenty years, and has owned and operated Lelevich Construction since 1997.Besides owning his own business, Len also works and volunteers with Cowlitz County Habitat for Humanity, and has taught vocational classes at Lower Columbia College. Len's reputation for qualty work, honesty, and professionalism has earned him a place among the most respected contractors in our region.
